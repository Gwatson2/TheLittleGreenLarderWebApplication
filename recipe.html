Recipe's

All recipe's were provided to us by the owner of The Little Green Larder.

When it comes to trying to live a sustainable life, some easy ways to do so are by shopping at a zero waste store (such as The Little Green Larder), buying loose fruit and veg and eating less meat. Here you will find some easy recipes that you can make from products you can purchase from The Little Green Larder which will help you to live a more sustainable life.
