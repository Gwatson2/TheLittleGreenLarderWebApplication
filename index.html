About Us


We are Dundee's first completely zero waste shop, meaning that nothing you buy from us should ever end up in landfill
thelittlegreenlarder offers a plastic free shopping experience, with zero waste alternatives to inspire us to create a greener future!

  • We aim to be affordable and offer zerowasteshopping that doesn't cost the earth
  • We sell wholefoods, driedfoods, bread, freshfruit and veg veganpickandmix, vegantreats, chocolate, cannedgoods and much more. We also have ecobeauty products, reusable items and refillable cleaning products
If you want to find out more come in and see what we have in offer and say hello!!

Dundee’s first zero waste store Little Green Larder open for business

Dundee’s first zero waste store has opened its doors.

The Little Green Larder, which is based on Perth Road and hopes to reduce plastic pollution, opened to the public yesterday.

The idea for the shop came from owner Jillian Elizabeth (pictured), who has worked on the concept for more than a year.

The shop will sell whole foods, dried and fresh food, as well as snacks and beauty products alongside zero waste alternatives for day-to-day items......
